# Adaptability

Forward-deployed work means the domain keeps changing under you: veterinary invoices one week,
pharmaceutical analytics the next. Get up to speed fast and be useful before you know
everything.

## Approach

- **Learn by building.** Get oriented by making something small and real, not by reading
  forever.
- **Abstract first.** Understand the end-to-end shape of a problem before diving into the
  details. That is what makes moving between domains cheap.
- **Sit with ambiguity.** Non-deterministic systems and half-defined problems are where the
  interesting work is. Do not wait for a perfect spec to start.

## Defaults

- You do not need to be the domain expert to be useful. Learn fast and ask the right people.
- Adaptable beats narrowly optimal when the ground keeps moving.
