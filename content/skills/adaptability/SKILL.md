# Adaptability

Forward-deployed work means the domain keeps changing under you. One project is veterinary
invoices, the next is pharmaceutical analytics. I am comfortable getting up to speed fast and
being useful before I know everything.

## How I approach it

- **Learn by building.** I get oriented by making something small and real, not by reading
  forever.
- **Abstract first.** I try to understand the shape of a problem end to end before diving into
  the details, which makes moving between domains easier.
- **Sit with ambiguity.** Non-deterministic systems and half-defined problems are where I do
  some of my best thinking.

## What I believe

- You do not need to be the domain expert to be useful. You need to learn fast and ask the
  right people.
- Adaptable beats narrowly optimal when the ground keeps moving.
