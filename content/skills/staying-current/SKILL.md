# Staying Current

## Approach

1. **Filter signal from hype.** Follow primary sources — papers, release notes,
   benchmarks — over hot takes.
2. **Prototype to learn.** Judge a new tool by building a small thing with it, not by
   reading threads about it.
3. **Adopt deliberately.** New is not the same as better. Weigh maturity, lock-in, and
   real gains before putting something in production.

## Habits

- Track releases from the labs and the tooling I rely on (LangChain, model providers).
- Keep a running list of techniques to try; revisit it, don't hoard it.
- Share what I learn with the team so knowledge compounds.

## Stack

`Applied research` · `Continuous learning` · `Tech radar`
